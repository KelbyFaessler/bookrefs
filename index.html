How it works

You give us the title and author of a book. Our software searches that book for every place another book is mentioned. In seconds, we give you a list of every single book cited, referenced, or even casually mentioned in the title you gave us.

You get a complete list of all these book references, plus the page number and surrounding paragraph for context. Easy to find what you're looking for.

Find your book

Maybe you just finished a fantastic book and want to further explore a topic. There was that one book the author mentioned that caught your eye but you don't remember the name. A really great book can be life changing. Don't leave your next one up to chance! Find it with our help.
